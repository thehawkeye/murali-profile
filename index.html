The Operator

I spent 27+ years scaling consumer tech businesses from tens of millions to billions. Long track record in leadership roles. At Xiaomi India, I led strategic projects, operations, offline sales, retail and public affairs across 10,000+ touchpoints, scaling market share to leadership and reinforcing trust in challenging times. At Myntra-Jabong (Flipkart Group), I ran a 400+ person organization in the midst at a strategic juncture. At eBay, I laid the foundational building blocks for e-commerce (intermediated payments, shipping, category management) and built categories from scratch across India, Philippines & Malaysia.

The Scholar

Now I bring that operational depth to the classroom and my research. My Doctoral Research at ISB studies how platforms create lock-in and I've lived it. My dissertation, "The Many Roads to Hotel California: The Ecosystem Vortex," bridges theory and practice.

The Investor

I back founders with the same playbook: operational rigor, consumer obsession, and long-term thinking in consumer tech. 10+ investments across D2C, healthtech, and petcare. Currently on the Investment Committee at D2C Super Angels Fund.
